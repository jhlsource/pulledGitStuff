Hey there I am Jun-Hon Lau,

Growing up in the picturesque village of Hebden Bridge, a constant intention throughout my life, has been towards how I can connect people to live their most joyful manifestation of their own, and I intend to do this through the use and application of digital technologies.

I have been interested in Digital Technologies, and their uses for a long time, my passions lie in the junction where craft and technique intercepts expression. With a unique creative mind, I dedicate my life to the service of humanity reaching its highest form of self-expression.

Current skill set:
