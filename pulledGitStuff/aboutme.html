About Me

"Let's start with “I know nothing”."

Hi. My name is Jun-Hon Lau and I am a local to Manchester. Growing up in the picturesque village of Hebden Bridge, a constant intention that has remained throughout my life, has been connecting people to living their most joyful manifestation of it. Current aptitudes include high levels of verbal comprehension, computer programming, ballet, pattern draughting and manufacturing garments, design to "tech-pack" and colour aptitude.

My passions lie in the junction where craft and technique intercepts expression. With a unique creative mind, I dedicate my life to the service of humanity reaching its highest form of self-expression.

Passions currently working towards include: mathematics, chemistry, computer science. Aspiring future spaces for expansion: health, travel, cello, art (ceramics), technical consultation in information/digital technologies.
